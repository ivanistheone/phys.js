Theory

Since we're learning physics here, we're going to have to see some math.

Earlier, we had a distance $d$, a velocity $v$, and a time $t$. But how do they all relate?

\begin{align*} t = \frac{ \Delta x }{ v } \\ v = \frac{ \Delta x }{ t } \\ \Delta x = v t \\ \end{align*}
